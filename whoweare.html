Who We Are

We are 3 Kode With Klossy scholars trying to make a difference in the world! We want to help other's be aware of their own waste production, and how they can try to lead a more sustainable lifestyle! Through this website, we hope to bring awareness to the wasteful lifestyles of the average person. Thanks for stopping by!
